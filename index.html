Narrative Knowing 1
The Questioning Mind 🤔

Descartes' cogito clicked instantly for me. Doubting is thinking, and thinking is existing. As a CS student working with low-level systems like AVX-512 assembly and a data analytics intern at the BSP, I naturally approach life as a system. The gym became my lab for self-discovery: Mead's generalized other, Cooley's looking-glass self, and Goffman's performance all play out while I blast Selena Gomez or Lady Gaga. But underneath my analytical, Western mindset, my family built the collective scaffolding that keeps me grounded.

Narrative Knowing 2
The Constructed Self 🧩

I am navigating six dimensions deliberately. I maintain a strict digital privacy strategy where my real personality and my cats, Moon and Leon, remain offline. My provider mindset is shaped by my father, aligning with Maslow's hierarchy to build a foundation first. While I approach the world empirically through data and code, my faith provides a moral foundation logic cannot replace. As Foucault suggests, I acknowledge power relations, but I do not surrender to them.
